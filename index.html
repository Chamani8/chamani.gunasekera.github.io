Chamani M. Gunasekera

Research Interest

I work on understanding the underlying physics (from cosmic chemical evolution to details of radiative transfer) of hot gaseous environments from optical to X-ray. I study the chemical evolution, the detail of radiative transport, and the microphysical processes in astrophysical gases. I am a collaborator of the Cloudy, a spectral synthesis software that simulates conditions in gaseous nebulae.
